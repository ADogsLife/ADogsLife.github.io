Services


Daycare

Doggie daycare work a lot like daycare for human babies. You drop your dog off in the morning on the way to work andd get back your dog when you are finished for your work. Depending on your dog's needs and the curriculum set out by the daycare facility, your dog may enjoy playing with other dogs, playing with people. and snuggling up on a nice bed or couch getting love attention to have his happy day.

Cost of Doggie Daycare

Requirements

All dog must meet the following requirement before any temperament testing is done:

  • Dogs that are 12 weeks or older.
  • Dogs in good healty(no communicable illnesses within the past 14 days.
  • Dogs free of fleas and ticks.
  • Dogs that have passed our initial behavioral evaluation
