Services


Dog Training Services

Our training school offers abedience training for any types of dogs including obeying commands like sit, stay, distance control and even walking on loose lead and being friendly with stranger. We also offers some specific sessions for the owners to help their pets gain better from the training. Furthermore, if you feel that your dog has mastered the basic obedience training and would like to focus on training for personal protection, we also ofer courses for your canines to pick up skills on being a bodyguard dog.

Benefits of Dog Training

  • Strengthen the bond between you and your dog
  • Become more healthy and strong.
  • Reduce stress
  • Overcome problem behaviors
